Professional Summary

I am currently involved in data and business intelligence projects focused on transforming fragmented business information into reliable decision-making systems. My work includes cleaning and standardizing Excel-based data sources, designing data pipelines for Power BI, organizing structured backups in Microsoft Fabric lakehouses, creating dataflows, and developing dashboards with high-value KPIs for senior management.

My professional interests are centered on data architecture, analytics, reporting automation, operational improvement, and digital transformation. I enjoy combining technical tools with business understanding to build practical and scalable solutions.

Experience Highlights

During my professional internship at MPM Rental, I contributed to the design and implementation of the company’s first Business Intelligence platform for the rental business. The project involved integrating dispersed Excel data sources, building cloud-based ETL processes, designing a relational analytical model, and creating executive dashboards in Power BI.

The final solution included automated data flows, integrated relational modeling, and validated financial and operational indicators. This project helped management move from fragmented manual analysis to centralized and faster access to business information.
